Our Story

More Than a Salon —
An Experience

Salon UnWINE & Boutique was born from a simple belief: every client deserves to feel like royalty. Located in the heart of Daytona Beach, we specialize in natural hair care — and we've built a reputation as the place where you come in a client and leave feeling like the best version of yourself.

Owned and operated by Queen, whose talent speaks for itself — we cater to all hair types, with a specialty in locs, braids, silk press, and protective styles. And when you're done in the chair, step over to our in-house clothing boutique.
