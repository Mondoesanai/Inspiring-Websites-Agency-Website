What You Can Expect

Both events are intentionally designed to go deep. Here is what each experience holds for you.

Extreme Marriage Challenge
What the Weekend Holds
🔥

Breakthrough Sessions

Guided sessions that bring hidden tensions to the surface in a safe, healing environment — and show you the path forward.

💬

Communication Rebuilding

Tools and exercises to help couples hear each other again — not just words, but the hearts behind them.

✝️

Faith-Rooted Foundation

Every session is grounded in Scripture and the covenant of marriage — reminding you why you chose each other.

🌅

Renewal Ceremony

A closing moment of recommitment and hope — a new chapter officially beginning.

Adventure Odyssey
What the Journey Holds
🏔️

Physical Challenges

Outdoor activities chosen to stretch you physically — hiking, challenges, and adventures that prove your strength to yourself.

🧭

Identity Workshops

Inner work sessions woven into the experience — unpacking who you are, what you carry, and what you are ready to release.

🌸

Sisterhood Bonds

You will leave with women who have seen you cry, cheer, and push through. These are not just event friendships — they are life ones.

✨

The Becoming Ritual

A closing ceremony honoring the version of yourself that showed up and the one you are choosing to become.
